This two-vehicle collision occurred early afternoon. It was clear at the time of the collision. The collision occurred at a four-leg intersection. The north/south roadway is five lanes, undivided, dry, level and made of asphalt.  The east/west roadway is two lanes undivided, dry, level and made of asphalt. The intersection is controlled by a on-color signal, which was working properly at the time. The north/south roadway is designated 56 kmph (35 mph). The east/west roadway is designated 40 kmph (25 mph).Vehicle one (V1) is a 1993 Toyota Camry 4-door sedan, driven by a thirty eight (38) year old unlicensed female.  V1 was traveling north in the number one lane. Vehicle two (V2) is a 2002 Honda Accord 4-door sedan, driven by a fifty two (52) year old female.  V2 was traveling west in the number one lane. As V1 entered the intersection, its front plane impacted V2's left plane, a corner to corner impact. V2 rotated clockwise and came to rest in the north crosswalk area in the northbound lanes. V1 continued northwest impacting a curb with its front plane and then a fence with its front plane. V1 came to rest facing northwest on the west curb.The driver of V1 is 38 year-old female. No interview was obtained for this driver.  The driver of V2  refused the interview.
