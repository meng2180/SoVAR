This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is undivided, straight and level. This roadway consists of two lanes in each direction and a center left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 56 kmph (35 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.Vehicle one (V1), a 1991 Toyota pickup, was southbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 2000 Honda Accord, was northbound in lane one. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. V2 traveled off the northeast corner of the intersection and the right side struck a hedge. Both vehicles were towed due to damage.The driver of V1, a fifty-three-year-old male, was traveling from a bar to his home nearby. This driver stated that he had spent the past couple of hours drinking beer at the bar and felt drunk when he left the bar. He further stated that he did not see V2 prior to the crash.
