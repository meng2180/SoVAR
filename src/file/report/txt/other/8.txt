This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The north/south roadway is straight, level and consists of two lanes in each direction and a center left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.Vehicle one (V1), a 2001 Toyota Echo, was northbound in lane three. Vehicle two (V2), a 1996 Dodge Caravan, was southbound in lane one. In the intersection, V1 turned left and the front struck the left side of V2. Both vehicles towed due to damage.The driver of V1, an eighteen-year-old female, was not interviewed. This driver stated to the police officer at the scene that she saw V2, but thought that she had time to make her turn.
