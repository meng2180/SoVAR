This two-vehicle crash occurred in a four-legged, traffic signal-controlled intersection. The east/west roadway is undivided, straight and level. The westbound approach lanes consist of one through lane, a right turn lane and a left turn lane. The eastbound approach lanes consist of two through lanes and a left turn lane. The speed limit on this roadway is 72 kmph (45 mph). This crash occurred during daylight hours with no adverse atmospheric conditions.Vehicle one (V1), a 2002 Lincoln LS, was westbound in lane three, the left turn lane. Vehicle two (V2), a 1998 Chevrolet Geo Tracker, was eastbound in lane two. V1 turned left in the intersection and the front struck the front of V2. Both vehicles were towed due to damage.The driver of V1, a seventy-six-year-old female, was on her way home from a country club. She was healthy, well rested and familiar with her vehicle. She travels on this roadway daily. This driver takes the medications Clorithizol, Plavix and Ceracolon. This driver stated that she was focused on the vehicle in front of her that was also turning left.
