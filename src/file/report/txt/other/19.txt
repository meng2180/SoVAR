This two-vehicle crash occurred at a four-way intersection that is controlled by traffic lights on colors. There are two eastbound and two westbound lanes that are separated by a left turn lane. There are two southbound and tow northbound lanes that are separated by a left turn lane. There is a raised median that the divided the lanes to the north of the intersection. The speed limit on both roadways is posted 56 kmph (35 mph). Both roadways were straight, level and constructed of bituminous asphalt. The roadways were dry and the sky was clear at the time of this morning daylight crash.  There was a possible sight line restriction at the northeast corner of the intersection. Vehicle one (V1), a 1994 Nissan Quest Van with one occupant, was traveling southbound in the number one lane approaching the intersection. Vehicle two (V2), a 2003 Ford F-350 4wd pickup with one occupant, was traveling westbound in the number one lane approaching the intersection. V1 entered the intersection on a red light and traveled across the path of V2. The impact forced V1 to rotate counterclockwise and travel through the intersection while rotating in a southwesterly direction. The vehicle came to rest in the eastbound number two-lane facing in a southwesterly direction. V2 came to a controlled stop to the north off the roadway west of the intersection. The driver of V1 was transported from the scene by EMS. V1 was towed due to damage; V2 was driven.The seventy one (71) year old male drove V1. The driver was not interviewed after several attempts to locate him.
