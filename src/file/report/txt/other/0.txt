	V1, a 1997 Chevrolet S-10 was traveling southbound on a two-lane, one-way, dry concrete highway in daylight conditions.  	V1 was traveling in lane two when a pedestrian walked into the roadway.  The driver of V1 did not see the pedestrian and contacted its front to him. V1, then came to final rest off the left side of the roadway.	The driver of V1 was a 74-year old male who had reported that he had been traveling 98-112kmph (61-70mph) prior to the crash.  He had no health related problems and had taken no medication prior to the crash.  He was rested and traveling to a restaurant.  He was wearing his corrective lenses that correct an unknown condition.  He did not sustain any injuries in the crash and refused treatment.
