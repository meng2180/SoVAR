The crash occurred in the eastbound lanes of a two-way roadway that is divided by a center median with three lanes in each direction.  It was daylight and clear and the ground was wet from a previous rainstorm.  The roadway was straight and level and had a posted speed limit of 48kmph (30mph). V1 was a 2000 Mercedes-Benz ML320 that was stopped for the Traffic control device when it was rear-ended by V2, a 1996 Toyota Corolla.
