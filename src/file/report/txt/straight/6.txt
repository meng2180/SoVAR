This crash occurred on a 4 lane undivided roadway slightly curving to the left prior to the crash site. The posted speed limit is 48 KMPH (30MPH) with no grade. The weather was clear and the roadway was dry. The crash occurred on a weekday evening.Vehicle 2, a 1999 Mercury Mystique, was traveling north on the four lane undivided roadway when it stopped to allow several southbound vehicle to pass before proceeding to make an intended left turn into a retail establishment's driveway. Vehicle 1, a 2003 Dodge Neon, was traveling in the same direction behind Vehicle 2. Vehicle 1 impacted the rear of Vehicle 2 with its front. Both vehicles came to rest in the roadway facing north.The Dodge Neon (Vehicle 1) was driven by a belted 18 year old female who was transported to an area hospital, treated and released. She stated she didn't know what went wrong as she didn't realize the vehicle in front of her had stopped. This driver stated she was not in a hurry, does not have any medical conditions and does not take any medication. This Vehicle was towed from the scene due to damage.
