The crash occurred on the eastbound lane of a two-way road with one lane in each direction.  The roadway was straight and level, and it was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions. There were no traffic control devices and the speed limit was 48kmph (30mph).Vehicle # 1 was a 2004 Mercury Sable that was traveling westbound.  The driver veered into the eastbound lanes and the front of Vehicle # 1 impacted the left side of a legally parked and unoccupied Ford E-Series Van. Vehicle # 1 came to a final rest when it impacted a fence on the sidewalk. The Mercury Sable (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 55 year-old female who sustained minor injuries and was transported to a local hospital.  The occupant in the front-center seating position did not sustain any injuries but was also transported to a local hospital.  The driver stated that she attempted to stop when she stepped on the accelerator instead of the brake pedal.  The Mercury Sable was towed due to damage and the driver declined to participate in the study.
