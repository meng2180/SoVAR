This crash occurred in the right lane of the northbound lanes of an undivided roadway with two lanes in each direction.  The roadway was straight and level, and it was cloudy and raining.  The roadway was wet and had a posted speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph). The crash occurred at a 4-way signalized intersection.  Vehicle #1 was a 1999 Toyota Camry that stopped for an emergency vehicle traveling westbound with the emergency lights on.  Vehicle #2 was a 2000 Nissan Sentra that was behind Vehicle #1 and could not stop in time.  Vehicle #2 rear-ended Vehicle #1.   The emergency vehicle was crossing over the intersection.A seventy-four year old male who did not sustain any injuries drove the Toyota Camry.  He stated that he saw the emergency vehicle trying to go through the intersection so he slowed down and came to a stop.  As he stopped, he was rear-ended by Vehicle #2.
