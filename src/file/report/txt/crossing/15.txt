The crash occurred in the intersection of a two-way north/south road with two lanes in each direction and an east/west road with one lane in each direction.  The roadways were straight and level and each had a speed limit of 48 kmph (30 mph).  It was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions.  The crash occurred in the morning hours on a weekday.Vehicle #1 was a 2004 Acura MDX that was traveling southbound in the right lane.  Vehicle #2 was 1995 Dodge Neon that was traveling westbound. At the intersection, the front of Vehicle #1 impacted the right side of Vehicle #2.    The Acura MDX (Vehicle #1) was driven by a 29-year old female who did not sustain any injuries.  She stated that she had come to a rolling stop at a red light and then when her light turned green, she accelerated into the intersection. She stated that Vehicle #2 came from nowhere.  The Acura was not towed.
