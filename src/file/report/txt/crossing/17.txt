The crash occurred in the intersection of a signalized one-way roadway and a two-way roadway.  The roadway was straight and level, and it was clear with no adverse conditions.  It was daylight and the roadway was dry and had a downhill grade of -2/61.Vehicle # 1 was a 1999 International M/H Truck that was traveling westbound on the middle lane of the roadway.  Vehicle # 2 was a 2002 Saturn SL that was traveling westbound on the right lane.  Vehicle # 1 was making a right turn at the intersection when he was impacted on the right side by the front of Vehicle # 1.The International truck (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 29 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries.  The passenger in the front right seating position also did not sustain any injuries.  The International truck was not towed due to damage.
