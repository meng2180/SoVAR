     The crash occurred in the westbound lane of a three-lane intersection.  Conditions were daylight on a weekend.  The westbound lane had a posted speed limit of 56 KMPH (35 MPH) and the northbound lane had a posted speed limit of 64 KMPH (40 MPH).     V1 was a 2002 Honda CRV traveling westbound in the right lane approaching the intersection with a green traffic control signal.  V2, a 2003 Dodge Durango, was traveling northbound in the left lane approaching the same intersection.  V2 went thru the red traffic control and the front of V2 struck the left side of V1 in the westbound lane.  V2 came to final rest facing northwest.  V1 continued on and struck a curb then rolled Â¼ turn onto its left side.  V1 came to final rest on its left side facing north.     V1 was driven by a 65-year-old male who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital.  The driver of V1 stated that he was on a "Sunday drive" with his wife, proceeded thru the intersection with a green traffic control signal, and was struck on the left side.  No avoidance actions were taken and the driver of V1 was traveling between 66 and 80 KMPH (41 and 50 MPH) at the time of impact.  V1 was towed due to damage.
