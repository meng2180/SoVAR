The crash occurred in the intersection of a southbound one-way and a westbound one-way roadway.   The roadway was straight and level, and it was clear with no adverse conditions.  It was daylight and the roadway condition was dry.Vehicle # 1 was a 1998 Ford Windstar that was traveling southbound on the right turn lane making a right turn when the front of Vehicle # 1 impacted the right side of Vehicle # 2, a Ford E-Series Van that was traveling on the same roadway and attempting to make a right turn from the left lane.The Ford Windstar (Vehicle # 1) was driven by a 64 year-old male who did not sustain any injuries.  The driver stated that he was making a right turn when the right side of Vehicle # 2 impacted the front of his vehicle.  The Ford Windstar was towed due to damage.
