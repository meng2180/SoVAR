The crash occurred in the early afternoon of a weekday in the westbound lanes of a divided two-way roadway with three lanes in each direction.  The roadway was straight and level and had a posted speed limit of 48kmph (30mph).  It was daylight and clear with no adverse conditions.V1, a 1997 Infiniti QX4, was traveling westbound in the right lane (lane one).  V2, a 1990 Ford Crown Victoria, was traveling westbound on the middle lane (lane two).  V2 attempted to make a right turn at the intersection the right side of his vehicle collided with the left front of V1.  V1 then went off the roadway to the right and then the front impacted a breakaway light pole.V1 was driven by a 34 year-old female who sustained minor injuries and was transported to a medical facility.  She was unable to be located for an interview, but she told the police on scene that she was going straight when V2 attempted to make a right turn at the intersection.  The Infiniti was towed due to damage.
