     The crash occurred in the northbound lane of a four-lane intersection controlled by a stop sign.  Conditions were daylight and dry in the morning on a weekday.  The northbound lane had a posted speed limit of 80 kmph (50 mph) and a 2% uphill grade. The westbound lane had a posted sped limit of 56 kmph (35 mph).     V1 was a 2003 Toyota Camry sedan stopped westbound at the stop sign in the first travel lane.  V2, a 2003 Nissan 350Z coupe, was traveling northbound in the second travel lane intending on going straight.  V1 was waved into the intersection by an unknown vehicle stopped in the 1st travel lane facing northbound.  V1 proceeded thru the intersection and the front of V2 struck the left side of V1 in the 2nd travel lane of the northbound roadway.  The driver of V1 lost consciousness and his vehicle traveled southbound hitting a curb and several signs traveling approximately 115 meters.  V1 came to final rest against a pole facing westbound.  V2 came to final rest facing westbound.     V1 was driven by a 57-year-old male who sustained moderate injuries and was transported to a local hospital.  He stated that he was stopped at the stop sign when an unknown vehicle waved him into the intersection.  The driver of V1 did not see V2.  The driver of V1 was traveling between 18 and 32 kmph (11 and 20 mph) at the time of impact.  V1 was towed due to damage.
